💡 FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Is my Google Drive™ data safe with Filerev?
Yes. Filerev connects through official Google OAuth with minimal permissions. Your files are never downloaded or copied, only metadata is read. Filerev is audited annually against OWASP ASVS and Google CASA security standards.

Will Filerev move or delete my files automatically?
No. Filerev never moves or deletes files without your explicit action. You review what Filerev finds, apply filters if you want, and decide what to clean up.

Can I recover files I delete with Filerev?
Yes. Deletions go to your Google Drive™ trash and stay recoverable for 30 days. The built-in Bulk Trash Restore tool can also restore every file in the trash at once.

Does Filerev work with Google Workspace Shared Drives?
Yes. Filerev scans Google Workspace Shared Drives on the Premium plan, as long as your Workspace plan includes Shared Drives.
